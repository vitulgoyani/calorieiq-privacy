🗑️ Delete Your CalorieIQ Account

You can permanently delete your CalorieIQ account and all associated data directly from within the app.

⚠️ Warning: Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. All your meals, weight logs, goals, and profile data will be permanently removed.

How to Delete Your Account

📱 Step 1: Open the CalorieIQ app on your device.
👤 Step 2: Tap the Profile tab at the bottom of the screen.
🗑️ Step 3: Scroll down and tap "Delete Account".
✅ Step 4: Confirm the deletion in the dialog. Your account and all data will be permanently deleted.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your account profile (name, email, goals)
  • All meal and food logs
  • All weight tracking entries
  • All notification settings
  • Your Firebase Authentication account
